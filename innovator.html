Dr. Timnit Gebru

Who is she?

Dr. Timnit Gebru is a computer scientist who studies artificial intelligence. She is known for her work on making AI systems fair and equal for everyone, and for advocating transparency in data-driven technologies.

Challenges

As a Black woman and immigrant, Gebru faced discrimination and underrepresentation in her field. In 2020, she was forced out of Google after raising concerns about bias in large language models, an event that sparked global conversations about ethics in AI.
